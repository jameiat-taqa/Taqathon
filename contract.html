Participation Agreement

Please review and sign to confirm your commitment to Taqathon.

Agreement Terms
1
This agreement defines the legal relationship between the Energy Association for Sustainable Development and participating teams in Taqathon.
2
It governs participation during the hackathon and grants the Association exclusive marketing rights for one year after signing.
3
Teams agree to submit original work, attend required workshops and sessions, and allow the Association to promote their projects.
4
If a project is adopted or invested in during the term, a 10% marketing fee applies.
5
The agreement is valid for one (1) year and may be signed electronically.
